Chandrayaan 2 Mission

Chandrayaan 2 was India's second lunar probe.
Chandrayaan 2 was launched on 22 July 2019

Chandrayaan 2

The primary objectives of the Chandrayaan-2 lander were to demonstrate the ability to soft-land and operate a robotic rover on the lunar surface.

A small error during the Chandrayaan-2 mission in 2019 led to its failure.
